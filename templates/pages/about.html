My Story: Where the Glide Began

Hello, I'm Brandon, the passionate waterman behind ADV eFoils. Founded in 2025, this business emerged from my lifelong passion for the water and sharing the exhilarating experience of foiling with everyone, regardless of wave conditions. I envisioned a world where the freedom of flight over water wasn't limited by tides or wind, but powered by sustainable, cutting-edge technology. ADV eFoils stands as a testament to my dedication and passion, offering premium eFoil experiences and lessons that redefine water adventure. I'm not just teaching eFoiling; I'm sharing a lifestyle of unparalleled freedom and connection with the ocean, one glide at a time.

My Mission: Sharing the Stoke of eFoil Flight

Eco-Conscious Adventure

I am committed to minimizing our environmental footprint. My eFoils operate silently and produce zero emissions, allowing you to experience the beauty of nature without disruption. I advocate for responsible tourism and ocean conservation.

Community & Accessibility

My goal is to build a vibrant community of eFoil enthusiasts. I offer comprehensive lessons designed for all skill levels, ensuring that everyone can discover the joy of eFoiling in a safe and supportive environment.

Innovation & Excellence

I'm always refining how I teach, using innovative methodsto ensure every lesson is clear, fun, and gets you flying faster. I pair this with top-tier eFoils from WayDoo for a safe and exhilarating experience, ensuring you're learning on the best equipment the industry has to offer right here on the Treasure Coast. My commitment is to deliver an unparalleled, high-quality adventure every time.

Passion & Personalized Experience

At the core of ADV eFoils is my deep passion for water sports and adventure. I am a certified instructor and fellow enthusiast dedicated to sharing my love for eFoiling and ensuring every lesson is a personalized, unforgettable ride.
