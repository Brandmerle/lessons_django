What is eFoiling?

eFoiling is the exhilarating sport of riding an electric hydrofoil board over water. Imagine effortlessly gliding above the shimmering surface of a tropical lagoon!

It's powered by a silent electric motor and a hydrofoil wing that lifts you, creating the unique sensation of flying. Controlled by a handheld remote, it's an eco-friendly adventure perfect for serene tropical waters, no waves required.

eFoiling Safety Tips

Your safety is our top priority for a worry-free tropical adventure. Always adhere to these guidelines:

  • Wear a Helmet: Essential for head protection.
  • Wear an Impact Vest/PFD: Provides buoyancy and protects your torso.
  • Leash Attachment: Always use the board leash to stay connected.
  • Know Your Surroundings: Be aware of other water users, obstacles, and depth.
  • Check Equipment: Before each ride, inspect your board, foil, and battery.
  • Start Slow: Master prone riding before attempting to stand up.
  • Learn from an Instructor: Professional guidance is highly recommended for beginners.
  • Respect the Water: Be mindful of weather conditions and local regulations.

Beginner eFoiling Steps

Follow these foundational steps to get comfortable and start flying effortlessly across the tropical waters:

  1. Safety Briefing & Equipment Familiarization

    Before launching into the blue, your instructor will guide you through all safety protocols and how to master your efoil's controls.

  2. Prone Riding (Lying Down)

    Begin by lying on your stomach, getting a feel for the board's balance and the remote's speed control. It's your first taste of the glide!

  3. Kneeling to Standing Transition

    Once you're comfortable prone, practice kneeling, then gradually push up to a standing position. Keep those knees soft and weight centered for smooth transitions.

  4. Controlling the Foil (Lift-off)

    As you build speed, a gentle shift of weight back will lift you above the water. Small, precise movements are your secret to sustained, magical flight.

  5. Gentle Turns & Speed Management

    Mastering turns comes with subtle weight shifts. Learn to smoothly adjust your speed, carving through the water with grace.

  6. Sustained Flight & Practice

    The more you practice, the more natural efoiling becomes. Enjoy the unparalleled sensation of flying, surrounded by the beauty of paradise.
